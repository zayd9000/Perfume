ERBIL CLIMATE WARNING:
Direct heat (45°C+) destroys fragrance molecules. Never store perfume in your vehicle. If the liquid looks cloudy or smells like rubbing alcohol, the oils have expired or it is a low-quality counterfeit.
PHYSICAL TRUTH CHECK:
• THE STRAW: Real dip tubes are nearly invisible. Thick tubes = Fake.
• THE CAP: High-end designers use heavy, precision-weighted caps.
• SPRAYER: Armani SWY atomizers are BLANK. If it has a logo, it's fake.
