ERBIL THERMAL PRESERVATION:
Erbil's heat (>45°C) triggers rapid molecular oxidation. Heat is the ultimate "Fragrance Killer." For maximum longevity, store bottles inside their original box within a dark, climate-controlled drawer. Never leave bottles in a vehicle for even 5 minutes.
ULTIMATE PHYSICAL TRUTH:
  • 1. THE SYNC: The Batch Code etched on the bottle glass MUST 100% match the code printed on the bottom of the box.
  • 2. THE GLASS: Luxury glass is high-clarity. If you see air bubbles or rough seams on the sides, it's a "Master Copy."
  • 3. ATOMIZER: Authentic sprayers have a clean, instant mist. Fakes often leak or have a visible spring inside the nozzle.
  • 4. THE CAP: Luxury caps (Dior/Chanel) are heavy. They should snap into place with a solid click, often being magnetic.
